It Ends With Us By
COLLEEN HOOVER

The first book that I’m going to review is my all-time favorite book where I can repeat reading this book many times without complaining. I’m in love with this book until i recommend it to everyone that they should give this book a try. "It Ends with Us" by Colleen Hoover is a contemporary romance novel that delves into themes of love, resilience, and the complexities of relationships. The story revolves around Lily Bloom, a determined woman who opens her own flower shop in Boston while trying to overcome her traumatic past. She meets Ryle Kincaid, a charismatic and ambitious neurosurgeon, and their romance blossoms. However, Ryle’s darker side gradually surfaces, forcing Lily to confront painful truths about love and abuse. At the same time, Lily reconnects with Atlas Corrigan, her first love, who was homeless during their teenage years. Atlas represents a safe haven and a connection to her past, which complicates Lily’s emotions and decisions. Through raw and emotionally charged storytelling, the novel addresses important topics such as domestic abuse, self-worth, and personal growth. Lily’s journey is one of courage and empowerment, as she strives to break free from toxic cycles for the sake of her own well-being and future. The book is both heart-wrenching and inspiring, making it a powerful read that leaves a lasting impact.

Why I said this book is my favorite book is because it sheds light on the importance of breaking free from toxic and abusive relationships. It emphasizes that prioritizing our own well-being and self-worth is not only a necessity but also an act of courage. Through Lily’s journey, the book powerfully conveys that loving and protecting ourselves must come first, as it sets the foundation for healthier relationships and a better future. Another reason I adore this book is its portrayal of the power of friendship. Lily’s bond with Allysa shows how meaningful connections can become a pillar of strength during difficult times. It reminds us that having a support system can make an immense difference when facing life’s challenges. Additionally, the story’s emotional depth resonates deeply. It doesn’t shy away from tough, real-world issues, and in doing so, it encourages reflection and awareness about the cycles of abuse and the strength it takes to break them. At the same time, it’s a story of hope, resilience, and personal growth, showing that even in the most painful circumstances, we can emerge stronger. Finally, I love how the book reminds us of the impact of our choices—not only for ourselves but for the generations to come. It’s a call to create a legacy of love, respect, and kindness. This combination of raw emotion, thought-provoking themes, inspiring messages makes "It Ends with Us" a book that truly stays with you long after turning the last page and definitely a worth book to read.
