It Starts With Us By
COLLEEN HOOVER

The second book that i’m going to review is my second favorite book and the continuation of It Ends With Us which is called It Starts With Us. "It Starts with Us" by Colleen Hoover is a contemporary romance novel and the much-anticipated sequel to "It Ends with Us." It follows Lily Bloom, now a single mother focused on raising her daughter, Emerson, while managing her flower shop after divorcing Ryle Kincaid. When she reconnects with Atlas Corrigan, her first love and now a successful restaurateur, their romance reignites amidst challenges, including Ryle's lingering jealousy and control. The story alternates between Lily’s and Atlas’s perspectives, offering insight into their emotions and struggles. Atlas’s backstory is explored further, introducing his estranged family and his younger half-brother, Josh, adding depth to his character. The book weaves themes of healing from trauma, self-love, and the beauty of second chances into a hopeful and heartwarming narrative. That said, for me personally, I found "It Ends with Us" more impactful, as I enjoyed Lily and Ryle’s chemistry in the beginning. Even though Ryle’s actions were harmful later, his initial charm and their connection left a strong impression, making "It Ends with Us" a more captivating story in my opinion.

I love "It Starts with Us" because it offers a heartfelt continuation of Lily and Atlas’s story, giving closure and hope after the emotional rollercoaster of "It Ends with Us." It beautifully emphasizes themes of healing and second chances, showing how love can flourish even after trauma. The alternating perspectives between Lily and Atlas allow readers to connect deeply with their characters and understand their personal growth. I also appreciate how Atlas's backstory is further explored, adding depth to his character and showcasing his resilience. Moreover, the book introduces new dynamics, like Atlas’s relationship with his younger brother, Josh, which highlights the importance of family and support. It’s a story that celebrates the strength it takes to rebuild, the courage to embrace love, and the beauty of finding happiness amidst life’s challenges. All these elements make "It Starts with Us" an uplifting and inspiring read.
